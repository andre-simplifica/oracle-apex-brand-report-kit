# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Positive behavior includes empathy, respectful feedback, accepting responsibility, focusing on what is best for the community, and welcoming different viewpoints. Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, sexualized language, insults, trolling, publishing private information, or conduct inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently ban contributors for behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful. Report incidents privately through the repository's security contact. Maintainers must respect the reporter's privacy and apply consequences fairly.
